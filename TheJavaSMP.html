Rules:

1 No spawnkilling. that means: if someone spawns, you kill this person over and over again. That is bannable.

2. No bad language.

3. Be nice to everyone. Show respect.

4. You can have a shop, but you need a license for that.

5. Don't be annoying.

6. Don't spam! It's very annoying. (Spamming is also breaking rule 5.)

7. Don't ask people to sleep in-game so it'll turn day. (It will also break rule 5.)

8. If you made a deal, then do it.

9. You can die, But don't die 5x to the same reason. That changes the server for you to Hardcore mode(*).

10. If you get banned, don't complain. We're sure it has a reason for your ban. You can also appeal! (Scroll down!)

11. No griefing.

12. Don't ask people for (free) stuff.

13. Don't ask people for money.


*Hardcore Minecraft: If you play Hardcore, You´re in survival, but in hardcore. There are no cheats allowed.

So if you die, you will never be able to respawn. Only to spectate the world.
